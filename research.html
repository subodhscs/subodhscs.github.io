Subodh Chandra Subedi

PhD Candidate in Mechanical Engineering

University of Wisconsin-Madison


I recently defended my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at the the University of Wisconsin-Madison.
I worked with Prof.Krishnan Suresh at the Engineering Representations and Simulation Lab and Prof.Dan Thoma at the Alloy Design and Development Laboratories. My PhD research was a culmination of numerical simulation and experimental verification on design for additive manufacturing.
