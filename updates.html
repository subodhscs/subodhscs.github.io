  • July, 2024
    Paper published in Additive Manufacturing Journal

    Paper titled 'Deformation constrained support-structure optimization for laser powder bed fusion' has been published in Additive Manufacturing Journal. The paper proposes a strategy to minimize the amount of frame-type support structures constrained by allowable layer-wise part deformation. Contributions of the paper:

    • Use of evolving frame-support for evolving geometries
    • Coupled system analysis where part and supports are analyzed in tandem
    • Multi-load Optimization:
      • Objective: minimize support material
      • Constraints:
        • Vertical Deformation to prevent recoater collision
        • Total Deformation for better geometric accuracy
        • Minimum and Maximum thickness of frame members for manufacturability
